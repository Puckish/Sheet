Greeves (Clockwork Chamberlain) Once attuned, this tiny (1' tall) winged butler loyally serves and advises its owner. It will flee/hide during any combat, but can carry up to 50lbs without tiring, and can store/dispense its owners currency (almost no limit) from a pocket dimension in its head. The Chamberlain can act on your behalf while you are away and has the Chamberlain Background at 8+its owners level for any checks it may need to make. As Lestin doesn't really "understand" money, Greeves handles payment for all goods and services for him as he breezes his way through life.
